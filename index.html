I'm Jinesh. I like to design and develop algorithms for problem-solving. I like to write scripts to automate my tasks so that my time is better spent doing the things I like.

I started automating tasks during my undergrad at Manipal Insitute of Technology. Right now, I work as a Software Engineer at Honeywell Technology Solutions Pvt. Ltd. I develop simulation software which help Mechanical Engineers develop power engines for aircrafts.

Apart from geeking out on technology, I spend my time playing sports and in activities like organizing blood donation camps, charity fund-raising events, and cloth collection drive along with their distribution among the needy. I started serving at Rotaract Club Manipal (100th Club in Rotaract International) and still am a proud rotaractor.
